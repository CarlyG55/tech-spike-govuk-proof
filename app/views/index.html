Automated eligibility

Two teams on GDS' Government Data Exchange programme spent several weeks exploring different ways automated eligibility checking between public services could work.

The prototypes are of two fictional software platforms called GOV.UK Proof and GOV.UK Life Events. Both prototypes uses scenarios where data is shared between different public sector organisations reduce the burdens and costs of eligibility checking.

View prototype code on GitHub



GOV.UK Proof

This prototype uses the scenario of a university giving a student proof they are entitled to a council tax exemption from a local authority.

GOV.UK Life Events

This prototype uses the scenario of the Department for Work and Pensions allowing other public sector organisations to get notified when a user successfully applies for child Disability Living Allowance, so that they may provide proactive services supporting families of children with disabilities.
