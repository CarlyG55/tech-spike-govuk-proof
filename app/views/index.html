Tech spike concept: GOV.UK Proof

Two teams on GDS' Government Data Exchange programme spent a week exploring one way eligibility checking between public services could work.

The prototypes are of a fiction government platform called GOV.UK Proof. It uses the scenario of a university giving a student proof they are entitled to a council tax exemption from a local authority.
